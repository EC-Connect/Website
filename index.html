Problem Characteristics

  • Students do not register with S.E.C. because of overwhelming registration process
  • Student projects and/or resources are not tracked
  • Students are not able to connect with other students and/or resources
  • Students are not aware of S.E.C. activities and/or events
  • Student visits to the S.E.C. are not logged
  • Students do not know how to ask for what they need
  • Students are too afraid to ask for what they need
